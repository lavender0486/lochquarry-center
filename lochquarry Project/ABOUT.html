Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is dedicated to providing unforgettable outdoor experiences for young people aged 6–18, offering a wide range of land‑based, water‑based and rope‑based activities across some of Scotland’s most stunning natural locations.

With sites at Ardgartan on the shores of Loch Long, Glentress Forest near Edinburgh and Strathyre by Ben Ledi, every adventure is set against a backdrop of breathtaking scenery. Our highly trained staff are passionate about helping young people build confidence, develop new skills and enjoy the outdoors safely. From busy weekends with Scouts and Guides to weekday programmes for school groups, Lochquarry is committed to creating a welcoming, supportive environment where every visitor can explore, learn and discover the excitement of outdoor adventure.

Our community is what makes Lochquarry Outdoor Centre special. From Scouts and Guides to school groups and local families, young people bring energy, teamwork and a love of adventure to every visit. Whether they’re tackling a climb, exploring a trail or sharing stories together, our community grows through friendship, support and a shared passion for the outdoors.

Our Guides section brings together helpful tips, inspiring stories and practical information to support every visitor’s adventure at Lochquarry. From packing and preparation advice to trail‑setting insights that reveal how our routes are created, these guides offer a deeper look into the outdoor experience. You’ll also find community stories that highlight the groups who make Lochquarry special, along with travel tips to make planning your visit easy. For those who want to explore even more, our full collection of articles provides engaging reads that capture the spirit of adventure at the centre.

Our story begins with a simple goal: to create a place where young people can discover adventure, build confidence and connect with the outdoors. Over the years, Lochquarry Outdoor Centre has grown from a small local initiative into a vibrant hub for land‑based, water‑based and rope‑based activities across some of Scotland’s most inspiring locations. Guided by passionate staff and shaped by the energy of Scouts, Guides, school groups and families, our journey continues to be driven by community, teamwork and a shared love of exploration. Lochquarry is more than an activity centre—it’s a story of growth, learning and unforgettable experiences.
